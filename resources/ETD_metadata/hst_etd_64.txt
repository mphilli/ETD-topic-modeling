$TITLE:
Fire on the Rim: A Study in Contradictions in Left-Wing Political Mobilization in Hong Kong 1967

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
Labor clashes, Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, Demonstrations

$ABSTRACT:
The following investigation will evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of the Cultural Revolution-inspired struggle campaign in Hong Kong, thereby reflecting on the appropriateness of the Chinese revolutionary model as a guide to popular advancement elsewhere, and on the extent and quality of leftist organizational efforts in a particularly potentially-revolutionary setting.
