$TITLE:
The Development Of Computer Programming Concepts And Problem-Solving Abilities Among Ten-Year-Olds Learning Logo

$DEPARTMENT:
Electrical Engineering and Computer Science

$KEYWORDS:
Computer science, Education, string-processing languages, problem-solving by children

$ABSTRACT:
This paper examines some empirical issues regarding development of problem-solving by children. The study surveys several semesters of children's LOGO programming, and isolates some concepts and skills which the children developed.--Introduction.
