$TITLE:
Venetian diplomacy and the treaty of Carlowitz, 1698-1699

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
Venetian diplomacy, Treaty of Carlowitz

$ABSTRACT:
The purpose of this study is to account for the failure of Venetian deplomacy at the Congress of Carlowitz.  That failure is linked directly to the political and economic policy of the Republic of Venice during the latter part of the seventeenth century...
