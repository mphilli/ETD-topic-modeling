$TITLE:
The Rise of the Old Orthodox Merchants of Moscow 1771-1894

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
Russia, Economy, Religious minority, Entrepreneurs, Orthodox Church

$ABSTRACT:
The Old Orthodox, those who remained faithful to the Orthodox Church as it was before the changes of the mid-seventeenth century, are the religious minority that played a noticeable role in the economy of Russia. ...This study will investigate the origins and the form of this entrepreneurial activity among the Old Orthodox families of Moscow.
