$TITLE:
Satire and Freedom: Sinclair Lewis, Nathanael West, and James Thurber

$DEPARTMENT:
Languages, Literatures, and Linguistics

$KEYWORDS:
satire, english literature, James Thurber, Nathanael West, Sinclair Lewis

$ABSTRACT:
My primary interest has been to examine the ways in which three authors – Sinclair Lewis, Nathanael West, and James Thurber – use fantasy in their satires and to determine the specific function it performs.
