$TITLE:
Socio-Economic Variables Associated with Political Change in Philadelphia: 1950-1965

$DEPARTMENT:
Political Science

$KEYWORDS:
Political science, Philadelphia, socio-economic, city government

$ABSTRACT:
The single dominant purpose of this study is to describe systematically some of the critical elements which comprise the political system of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The fifteen-year period of 1950-1965 is used to compare and contrast socio-economic characteristics in the city during this time.
