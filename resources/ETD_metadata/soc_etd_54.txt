$TITLE:
American Museums, 1932-1965: A Test Case for the Comparative Analysis of Organizations

$DEPARTMENT:
Sociology

$KEYWORDS:
Organizational theory, Negotiated order, Compatative analysis

$ABSTRACT:
The purpose of the study is to establish the basis for a comparative analysis of organizations and to complete a study on this basis. By doing so we will establish a test case for our techniques and help to define the limits for this important area in organizational history.
