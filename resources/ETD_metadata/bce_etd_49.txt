$TITLE:
Thermal instability and heat transport of a layer of non-newtonian fluid

$DEPARTMENT:
Biomedical and Chemical Engineering

$KEYWORDS:
thermal instability, heat transport, non-newtonian fluid

$ABSTRACT:
The object of the present investigation is to study, primarily by experimental means, the stability, free convection and heat transfer of a horizontal layer of non-Newtonian fluid heated uniformly from below.
