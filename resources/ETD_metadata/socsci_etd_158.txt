$TITLE:
Joseph F. Guffey, New Deal politician from Pennsylvania

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
Joseph F. Guffy, American history

$ABSTRACT:
The honorable Joseph F. Guffey, a one-time Wilsonian Democrat, ex-Senator, political "boss," ex-New DEaler and oil promoter was born in a family home at Guffey's Landing (later re-named Guffey Station), on the Youghiogheny River in Westmoreland County, Pennsylvania, on December 29, 1870...After attending Princeton University Preperatory School, young Joe entered Princeton in 1890.  While there, Guffey met professor Woodrow Wilson for whom he developed an admiration amounting ot hero-worship which was to last a lifetime...
