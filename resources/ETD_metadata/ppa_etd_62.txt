$TITLE:
Toward A Paradigm Of Future Public Administration: A Delphi Exercise Aimed At Defining The Scope And Methods Of Public Administration From 1973-1990

$DEPARTMENT:
Public Administration

$KEYWORDS:
Public administration

$ABSTRACT:
This study attempts to channel intuitive thinking, intentionality, and the subjective values of authorities in public administration, and in related fields, toward the prediction of a paradigm of future public administration. A Delphi exercise is used as the means for obtaiing the best judgments from scholars about the future scope and methods of public administration.
