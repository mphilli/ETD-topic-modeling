$TITLE:
Sam Jong Dong: A South Korean Village

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
Korea, Historical setting, Social behavior, Province of Kyongsang Namdo, Religious phenomena, Technology

$ABSTRACT:
Part I deals with introduction and the historical setting of Sam Jong Dong in the Province of Kyongsang Namdo in southeastern Korea; Part II deals with the social behavior seen in the village; Part III is concerned with religious phenomena; Part IV with technology; and Part V pertains to interpretation.
