$TITLE:
Operation 'Frantic' And The Search For American - Soviet Military Collaboration, 1941-1944

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
History, United States, Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, military coordination, bombing

$ABSTRACT:
This study attempts to appraise the primary instance of US/USSR military cooperation in World War II (direct flights from Italy to the Ukraine, with bombing enroute), and to investigate the optimism which this shuttle-bombing project inspired.--Pages 2-3.
