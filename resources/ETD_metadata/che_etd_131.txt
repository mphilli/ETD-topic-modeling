$TITLE:
Cyclopropane Geometry Effects in Rigid Cyclopropylaromatic Systems

$DEPARTMENT:
Chemistry

$KEYWORDS:
organic chemistry, nitration

$ABSTRACT:
Adding to the discussion of Nitration of aromatic systems, this thesis examines the reaction mechanism(s) and nitrating species responsible for the ortho effect (the formation of large amounts of ortho-nitro product in certain substituted benzenes by the action of nitric acid/acetic anhydride).
