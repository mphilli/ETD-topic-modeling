$TITLE:
Aristotle's 'Poetics' Read As A Reply To The Platonic Indictment Of Poetry

$DEPARTMENT:
Philosophy

$KEYWORDS:
Philosophy, aesthetics, poetry, Plato, Homer

$ABSTRACT:
This paper is intended to contrast  Aristotle's 'Poetics' with the Platonic indictment of 'poetry.' This includes consideration of Aristotle's definition of tragedy, and comments on Homer.--Page [1].
