$TITLE:
France, the United States, and the Lesseps Panama Canal: Renewed Rivalry in the Western Hemisphere, 1879-1889

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
Sphere of influence, Territorial expansion, Monroe Doctrine, Franco-American relations

$ABSTRACT:
My study is concerned with the quite unequal efforts of France and the United States each to create for itself a dominant position on the Panamanian isthus during the period -- 1879-1889 -- in which Lesseps attempted to construct a canal.
