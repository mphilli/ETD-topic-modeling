$TITLE:
Diels-Alder Reactions of Thiete Sulfone

$DEPARTMENT:
Chemistry

$KEYWORDS:
Organic chemistry, heterocycles, Diels-Alder

$ABSTRACT:
Discusses and reviews research involving the Diels-Alder reaction, or diene synthesis reaction, which is the most general reaction of for the synthesis of six-membered carbocyclic and heterocyclic ring systems in organic chemistry.
