$TITLE:
The reaction between tributylphosphine dibromide and alcohol

$DEPARTMENT:
Chemistry

$KEYWORDS:
tributylphosphine dibromide, organic chemistry

$ABSTRACT:
Alkyl halides are commonly prepared by reacting alcohols with HX, PX5, PX3, and SOX2 (X=halogen).  However, each of the reagents has its shortcomings and not one is completely general for converting all types of alcohols, regardless of structure, to their respective halides...
