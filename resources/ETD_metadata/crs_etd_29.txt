$TITLE:
Recurrent melodic structures and libretto continuity in Mozart's 'Die Zauberfloete'

$DEPARTMENT:
Music

$KEYWORDS:
Music, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, melody, librettos,  "Zauberflote"

$ABSTRACT:
This study attempts to define the scope of recurrent melodic structure in "The Magic Flute", the dramatic context of the recurrences, and the pertinence of these factors to the reading of the libretto as a dramatic continuum.--Preface.
