$TITLE:
The Industriels Forains of Brittany, an Itinerant Community

$DEPARTMENT:
Anthropology

$KEYWORDS:
Forains, carnival workers, fetes, itinerant workers, France, fairs

$ABSTRACT:
This paper examines the society of a unique, small  subculture in France of the forains (carnival or fair entrepreneurs) in Brittany. Interactions with the larger French culture are discussed, as the societal makeup of their own subculture, as well as their daily life and the continued existence of this culture.
