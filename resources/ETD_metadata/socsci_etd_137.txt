$TITLE:
Watchman of the Peace: The United Nations Secretary-General and the Prevention of International Armed Conflict

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
Armed conflict, International law, International relations

$ABSTRACT:
The purpose of this dissertation is to examine and evaluate the present, and potential, "diplomatic means" available to the Executive Office of the Secretary-General to prevent conflict. Special attention will be devoted to the Constitutional powers and responsibilities of the Secretary-General under Article 99 of the Charter of the United Nations.
