$TITLE:
Wyoming: A Study Of John Franklin And The Connecticut Settlement Into Pennsylvania

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
History, Susquehannah Company, Connecticut, Pennsylvania, American politics, interstate relations

$ABSTRACT:
This paper is the first full study of John Franklin, "the Hero of Wyoming [Pennsylvania]", who was a man of considerable local influence from the U.S. Revolution until the Presidency of Andrew Jackson.--Preface.
