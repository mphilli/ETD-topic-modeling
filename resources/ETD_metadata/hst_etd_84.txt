$TITLE:
Nietzsche And Wagner: Their Relationship

$DEPARTMENT:
History

$KEYWORDS:
History, Richard Wagner, Friedrich Nietzsche, German composers, German philosophers, influence

$ABSTRACT:
Discusses the relationship between Wagner & Nietzsche, and their mutual influence upon each other. Includes an attempt to address the question of why after seven years of friendship, a bitter enmity arose between them.--Preface.
