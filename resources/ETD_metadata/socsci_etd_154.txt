$TITLE:
The Spatial And Ecological Characteristics Of The Diffusion Of Christian Science In The United States: 1875-1910

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
Religion, historical geography, American studies

$ABSTRACT:
This dissertation looks at Christian Science in its spatial context, and attempts to discover some of the factors which were most important in influencing the distribution of this Church in the United States.--Preface.
