$TITLE:
The R.A. Smith Milling Company, 1889-1955: A case study of the entrepreneur and a small family business

$DEPARTMENT:
Social Sciences

$KEYWORDS:
Economic history, entrepreneurial history, gristmill, feed industry, family-run business

$ABSTRACT:
This case study of a small, family business covers a period of over sixty years, and attempts to describe the development of the company, the industry of which it is a part, and the setting in which it operates. Particular attention has been paid to the entrepreneurs and their roles in this development.--Preface.
