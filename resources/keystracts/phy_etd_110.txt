braneworld cosmology extra dimensions inflation preheating cosmic acceleration has come to be a standard and perhaps  required ingredient in our current understanding of the universe in the early  universe under the name of inflation a phase of accelerated expansion is used  to solve many problems with the standard hot big bang cosmology in the late  universe cosmic acceleration seems to best explain a wide variety of  observations in both cases we lack a complete theory of what drives cosmic  acceleration in this thesis i discuss some open issues in our understanding of  cosmic acceleration both in the early and late universe
