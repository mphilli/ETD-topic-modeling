 this study examined the socialization process which occurred early in the beginning elementary teacher's career socialization and reference group theory provided the overarching framework for the study in addition a number of constructs found helpful to earlier researchers organizational socialization perspective problematic situations and situational adjustment were usedthe intent of this study was to generate hypotheses not to test therefore in order to build grounded theory glaser and strauss 1967 a design that was open-ended was used while data collection relied on unstructured interviews with teachers in their first second and third year of teaching eventually fifteen teachers from different school districts were interviewed
