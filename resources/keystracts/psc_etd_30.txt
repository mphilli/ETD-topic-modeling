institutional changes that included the rise of the civil rights movement and professionalized interest groups the emergence of the united nations and a corresponding increase in nongovernmental organizations ngos and shifts in authority and influence among national political institutions that weakened pre-existing ties between women's civic groups and bureaucratic agencies of the federal
